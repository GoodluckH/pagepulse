‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does website monitoring work?

PagePulse checks your target URL at regular intervals and compares the content to previous checks. When something changes, you get an instant notification via email or webhook.

Can I monitor any website?

Yes! You can monitor any publicly accessible webpage - product pages, job boards, competitor sites, news articles, government pages, and more.

What are CSS selectors?

CSS selectors let you monitor specific parts of a page instead of the whole thing. For example, use ".price" to only track the price element, ignoring ads and other changes.

How fast will I get alerts?

Alerts are sent within seconds of detecting a change. Check frequency depends on your plan: daily (Free), hourly (Pro), or every 5 minutes (Team).

Is there an API?

Yes! PagePulse has a full REST API. Create monitors programmatically, trigger checks, and integrate with your existing workflows. View API docs ‚Üí
